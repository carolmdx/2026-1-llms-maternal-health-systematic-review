# (POS-2026-1) Large Language Models in Maternal Health — Systematic Review

This repository contains all materials, artifacts, and documentation used to conduct the systematic literature review titled:

**"The Use of Large Language Models in Maternal Health: Implications for Clinical Practice, Education, and Decision-Making Among Healthcare Professionals"**

---

## Objective

This study aims to analyze how Large Language Models (LLMs) are used by healthcare professionals in maternal health and to identify their applications, benefits, limitations, and implications for clinical decision-making and patient safety.

---

## Methodology

The review follows a structured systematic review protocol, including:

- Defined research question and PICOC
- Search strategy across multiple databases
- Inclusion and exclusion criteria
- Quality assessment of selected studies
- Data extraction and synthesis
